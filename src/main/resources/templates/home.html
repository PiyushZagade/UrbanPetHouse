About Us

Welcome to UrbanPetHouse – where your pet's happiness, comfort, and health come first! Founded by passionate pet lovers, our mission is to bring compassionate, reliable, and professional care to your pets right in the comfort of their own homes. We understand that every pet is unique, with individual needs, personalities, and routines. That’s why we tailor each visit to ensure your pet receives the attention and care they deserve.

Our team consists of highly trained pet caregivers and animal behavior specialists dedicated to creating a fun, safe, and stress-free experience for your furry friend. We offer a range of personalized services, including home visits, walks, grooming, overnight stays, and more. Whether you’re at work, on a trip, or just need an extra hand, we’re here to give your pet companionship, exercise, and love.
